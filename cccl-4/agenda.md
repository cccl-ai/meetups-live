## How It Works

**Speakers (6)** — 10 min demo or talk. You get a title slide with your name, topic, and QR code.

**Introducers (6)** — One per speaker. ~90 seconds to introduce yourself and the speaker.

**Lightning intros (5)** — 90 sec each with a slide showing your name, what you do, and a QR code.
