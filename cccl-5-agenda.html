How It Works

Speakers (6)

10 min demo or talk. You get a title slide with your name, topic, and QR code. You can add more slides for your talk.

Introducers (6)

One per speaker. You get ~90 seconds and your own slide to talk about yourself and your work, then introduce the speaker.

Lightning Intros (5)

90 sec each. You get a slide with your name, what you do, and a QR code (LinkedIn, website, or project link).
